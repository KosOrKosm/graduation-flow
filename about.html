WHAT IS GRADFLOW?


GradFlow makes it easy to create graduation flowcharts for students. We want to help students visualize their graduation paths. Create your graduation flowchart, print it, and hang it up in your room! Come back to GradFlow every semester and update your flowchart using our easy to use interface! Don't let prerequisite classes get the best of you (it did for some of us)!
